Service & Maintenance Application

Fortress Data And Analytics' comprehensive solution, ‘Enterprise Service and Maintenance Solution– eSAM’, is an application to carry out Field Service and Maintenance activities like Service Call Assignments, Service Call Logging, Scheduled Maintenance, Call based Servicing, Site Survey, Equipment Health Data Capture and Collection, for In-premises installations and facilities, Manufacturing units, Large Production setups, Engineering industries, Hospitals, Hotels etc. It is of most use to the field maintenance staff and the field service engineering teams. Providing clients with valuable and competent service and maintenance as per defined Standard Operating Procedures (SOP) or as per companies’ standardized compliance is the USP of eSAM.

How does eSAM ensure Service Level Compliance and Maximized RoI?

  • Real time Access to Information
  • Reduced Job Completion Cycle
  • Increases field performance by 40%
  • Eliminate redundancy, paperwork and scheduling time benefiting to save 30% time from administrative tasks and logistics
  • Increase service lead capturing
  • Third party integration with legacy systems and ERP

  • Uniform user-friendly interfaces for web portals and mobile applications
  • Maximum features and reports of all modules available on the mobile app

eSAM - The Process Flow

Display customer data & service call details on mobile device

View today’s visit planning

View visit sequence on map as per visit plan

Select customer, view call details and perform necessary servicing

Re-assign call to another engineer / reschedule visit for various possible reasons

Update visit summary and call completion status (pending, done, etc.)

Take customer’s signature and feedback on visit completion

Back office data synchronization on continuous basis or in batch
